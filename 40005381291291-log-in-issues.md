# Log In Issues

### Incorrect Log In

If you are trying to log in to your Parallel account and receive the error message:

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40044104747547)

Please check and take note of the information below:

**✓**Make sure you are using the email address associated with your Parallel account

**✓**Make sure there are no misspellings in your email address or password

**✓**Reload the Pathway landing page and try logging in again

If this does not resolve the issue, please click**Reset Password** to reset your password.

### Other Log in Issues

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40044082365979)

If you are trying to log in to your Parallel account and receive a different error message, please email [support@parallellearning.com](mailto:support@parallellearning.com) with the username/email address that you are attempting to log in with and the error message you received.
