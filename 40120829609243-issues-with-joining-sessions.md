# Issues with Joining Sessions

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40120829594267)

If you are unable to join the session lobby or join the session itself, you may be experiencing one of the following issues:

### ***Using an old version of your Internet Browser***

Updating to the latest version ensures that your internet browser works smoothly with Parallel’s sessions.

* [Update Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/update/)
* [Update Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204416)

### ***Firewall Permissions are not configured correctly***

Your IT team will be able to update your school’s firewall permissions to the correct configuration. [Please share this guide with your IT Team](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/en-us/articles/20457820922011-Parallel-Firewall-Configuration-and-Testing-Guide), as it has specific instructions on how to set up the firewall to support Parallel sessions.

### ***Audio/video app permissions***

Your computer may not have the right permissions enabled for you to use your camera or microphone on your internet browser app. To ensure that the camera/microphone permissions are set correctly, please follow the instructions listed:

* [Windows: Camera](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/manage-app-permissions-for-your-camera-in-windows-87ebc757-1f87-7bbf-84b5-0686afb6ca6b)
* [Windows: Microphone](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/turn-on-app-permissions-for-your-microphone-in-windows-10-94991183-f69d-b4cf-4679-c98ca45f577a)
* [Mac: Camera](https://support.apple.com/guide/mac-help/control-access-to-your-camera-mchlf6d108da/mac)
* [Mac: Microphone](https://support.apple.com/guide/mac-help/control-access-to-the-microphone-on-mac-mchla1b1e1fe/mac)

Your internet browser (e.g., Google Chrome, Safari) should be the app that you enabling permissions for.

### ***Audio/video browser permissions***

Your internet browser may not have the right permissions enabled for you to use your camera or microphone on the Parallel website. To ensure that the camera/microphone permissions are set correctly, please follow the instructions listed for the internet browser that you are using:

#### **Google Chrome**

1. Click the session link
2. In the address bar on the left, click the **Lock icon** to display device permissions.
3. Make sure Camera and Microphone are set to **Allow**.
4. Click the Lock icon to close the pop up.
5. **Refresh** your browser window

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40120829596059)

#### **Safari**

1. Click on the session link
2. Click **Safari** in the upper left hand corner, and then click “*settings for telehealth.parallellearning.com…*”
3. Click next to “Camera,” Microphone,” and “Screensharing” to change the setting for each to **Allow.**

## **⚠️ Common Errors ‼️**

Below are the common errors and  you may encounter while joining a session:

| **Issue** | **How to troubleshoot** |
| --- | --- |
| Appointment link is not valid | Appointment-specific links are only valid the day of the appointment.  Please ensure you are signed in as the correct user and accessing the correct appointment, and try again |
| Appointment Fetch Error | Telehealth is not pulling out the information from Pathway so this may be a Pathway problem or a potential bug. Follow the instructions indicated on the error message |
| Media Device Error | Computer might be handling multiple tasks, possibly with another program or site already utilizing the microphone or camera  Close the conflicting programs or website and refresh the Telehealth session page |
| Connection Error | This is a firewall issue or a problem on the network quality of the school  Do speed test at <https://www.speedtest.net/>and send results to [support@parallellearning.com](mailto:support@parallellearning.com) to rule out problem with Internet connection  Go to <https://telehealth.parallellearning.com/join> and enter the 5-digit session code which you can find on the Daily Session Summary or calendar invite  You may also try: - Logging out of your account and signing back in  - Closing the search engine  - Restarting your device |
