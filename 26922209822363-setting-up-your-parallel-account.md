# Setting Up Your Parallel Account

The Parallel account setup process begins with a welcome email from the Parallel team. Once you receive this email, account setup happens in just a few steps. If you run into any issues during the account setup process, please reach out to [support@parallellearning.com](mailto:support@parallellearning.com) for assistance.

### Setting Up Your Parallel Account

1. The welcome email you receive from the Parallel team is the first step in the account setup process and the message you receive will look something like this:

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/26922209815707)

2. Please follow the directions in this email and begin by clicking the **Set Password** link.

3. Clicking on the **Set Password** link brings you directly to the account setup page – enter your password and click **Submit.**

**![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/26922195599643)**

4. If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking **Reset Password** at the bottom of the **Parallel login page** at [app.parallellearning.com](https://app.parallellearning.com/). To learn more about the process on how to reset your password, you may visit this [article](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/en-us/articles/39819386717211).

**![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/26922209817883)**
