# Navigating Pathway - The Basics

Upon logging in Pathway, you will see the following tabs:

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40353607209627)

The **Calendar** tab allows you to navigate the provider's calendar and visits plotted.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40353607213595)

Click on the **calendar entries** to see details of the visit.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40357518538395)

Click on the **month** or **left and right arrows** to toggle between the weeks or choose the month you would like to view.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40355079093787)

Switch between weekly views, monthly views, or appointment list by clicking on the **Week** dropdown and choosing among options below. Tick or untick to show cancelled sessions, weekends, consult sessions, and meetings.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40353654136603)

Click on the ![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40355079096347) icon and choose your preferred timezone.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40355079104027)

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40355058715931) will bring you back to the current date.

The **Students** tab allows you to see a list of the students and their relevant information.

Search for a student by typing in the name beside ![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40356397720475)

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40356397724699)

Click on the **Student Name** to see Student Information, Documents, Goals, and Progress. You will see the Information tab by default.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40481208971035)

The **Documents** tab will show relevant documents of the student.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40490741385115)

The **Goals** tab will show student goals, when they were started, achieved, and the average objective time. Click on ![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40490741393435) to show or hide pinned, achieved, or archived goals.

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40490741389339)

The Progress tab will show student progress, which can be filtered by Provider and Date Range. You may opt to show or hide archived and achieved objectives, no-note appointments, and indirect time details by clicking on ![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40490741393435)

Download this data by clicking on ![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40490741394331)

![](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40490741395611)

Click on the **Help** tab to be redirected to the [Help Center](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/en-us/sections/10486679387675-FAQ) articles.
