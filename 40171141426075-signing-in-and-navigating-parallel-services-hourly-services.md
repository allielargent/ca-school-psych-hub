# Signing in and Navigating Parallel Services - Hourly Services

## **Welcome to Parallel!**

Parallel uses an online video platform and suite of tools to deliver remote services. As a Parallel session facilitator, you will generally support students by:

1. Communicating with Parallel provider(s) about student sessions and scheduling

2. Supporting students with logging in to access Parallel services

3. Supporting students during their sessions as needed

The level and exact nature of facilitator support will depend upon various student-centered factors - the Parallel team is here to support you at every step of the way! More in depth details on how you can best support your students is [available here](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/en-us/articles/43296632998683-Facilitator-Guide).

If you haven't yet set up your Parallel account, please visit [the guide](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/en-us/articles/26922209822363-Parallel-Pathway-Setting-Up-Your-Parallel-Account) that walks you through this process before proceeding. **If you run into any issues with setting up or accessing Parallel, please reach out to**[**support@parallellearning.com**](mailto:support@parallellearning.com)**for assistance.**

**💡 Tip** Additional Note: The Parallel team recommends using the Google Chrome web browser to access Parallel.

*Both video and written guides are provided below to walk you through the different aspects of Parallel services.*

**Signing Into Parallel**

1. Parallel services can be accessed at
<app.parallellearning.com>

2. To log in, enter your email and the password you set during the account
setup process and click **Sign In**. For easier access in
the
future, bookmark this page!

![Screenshot-1.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40198263651611)

3. If you've forgotten your password or need to set a new one, click
**Reset Password.**

![2.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40198250982171)

Accessing the Session Calendar. Click here to expand

**Accessing the Session Calendar**

Upon clicking **Sign In**, you will be brought to your calendar
of sessions - there are multiple settings you can change to customize
the
way the calendar displays.

1. To change the sizes displayed on your calendar, click the
**Zoom Out** and **Zoom In** buttons.

![3.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40171741775003)

2. To change between a daily view of the calendar and a weekly view,
click
on the dropdown menu that displays **Day** or
**Week** and make the desired selection.

![4.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40171741779227)

3. To change the timezone the calendar is displayed in, click the
**timezone display** beside the date of the week
and select the appropriate timezone.

## **Viewing and Joining Sessions: Video Guide**

## **Viewing and Joining Sessions: Written Instructions**

Click here to expand

1. Once your calendar is configured to your liking, any session already
scheduled
will be displayed. Click on any session to:

- View the full names of any students scheduled to attend a session

- Confirm the status or reason of cancelation of a session

- Join a session or copy a session link

2. Parallel providers classify sessions using different statuses that
are
marked and color-coded on the calendar.

- Sessions that occur as expected are marked **Occurred**
and
appear green on the calendar - You can click on Occurred sessions to
view
the timeframe of attendance for each student

![6.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40197487605147)

- **Canceled** sessions appear gray on the calendar - You
can
click on a canceled session to confirm the reason for cancelation

![7.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40197487607579)

- No-Show sessions are marked **No-Show** and appear red
on
the calendar

![8.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40197487609115)

- Sessions that do not have a status and appear blue on the calendar
are
either upcoming or have not yet been marked by the provider

![9.png](https://help.parallellearning.com/hc/article_attachments/40197487612955)

3. To join a session, click on the upcoming session you wish to join
on behalf
of a student on the session calendar and click
**Join Session**.

  

**ℹ️ Note**
Note: It’s important to allow your web browser to access the camera and microphone on each device used to access Parallel. Please select allow any time Parallel requests access to the camera and microphone.

4. After clicking Join Session, you will be brought to a
**Telehealth** page. On this page, you should see a live
camera
feed. If you see a black square or an error message, double check to
make
sure nothing is obstructing your camera and follow any troubleshooting
instructions
on your screen. You can confirm the date and time of the session on this
screen to make sure you're in the right place at the right time. To proceed
to the waiting room, select the student you are joining on behalf of
under
**Select your student account.**

5. The session will take a moment to load before you enter into the waiting
room. It may take some time for the provider to let you into the session
depending on when you join.



**ℹ️ Note**If you are using session codes to support students with logging into sessions, have students bookmark <https://telehealth.parallellearning.com/join>on their own devices and send them the code for their daily session(s) provided in the Daily Session Summary email. Each session code can also be found on the Join Session button!
