# Hub ↔ Help Center Relevance Map

Source: `CA_School_Psychologist_Hub_v3.html` (2,599 lines) mapped in full against the synchronized Zendesk Help Center (`index.json` / `INDEX.md`, 515 articles: 25 Parallel Help Center + 490 Parallel Provider Portal).

**Key framing finding (applies to every row below):** the Hub's existing content is almost entirely California **clinical/legal** guidance (statutes, eligibility criteria, IDEA/CA Ed Code citations). The Help Center is almost entirely Parallel's **operational/platform SOPs** (how to use Pathway, Report Writer, billing, scheduling, service lines). These two content types barely overlap topically even where they overlap in subject — e.g., the Hub's FBA guide is about clinical function-based assessment under CA law; the Help Center's FBA article is about how Parallel bills and scopes FBA work. This is why most rows below say **link only** or **add SOP note** rather than "replace": the two sources answer different questions and both are needed.

Legend for **Treatment**: `LINK` = link only · `SUM` = short summary + link · `INCORP` = incorporate short stable language directly · `FLAG` = flag for human review · `GAP` = no matching article exists (see Gap Report)
